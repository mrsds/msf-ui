### How do I turn on a layer?

1. Open the **MAP LAYERS** widget in the upper right corner of the map.
2. Click on the switch toggle just to the left of the layer title.

### Keyboard Shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|:---|---:|
| `Arrow-Up` | Zoom date slider towards days |
| `Arrow-Down` | Zoom date slider towards years |
| `w` | Pan map north |
| `s` | Pan map south |
| `a` | Pan map west |
| `d` | Pan map east |
| `Shift` + `w/a/s/d` | Fast map panning |
